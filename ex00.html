Problem 1
Multiples of 3 or 5
If we list all the natural numbers below 10 that are multiples of 3 or 5, we get 3, 5, 6 and 9. The sum of these multiples is 23.
Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000. answer = sum([num for num in range(1000) if num % 3 == 0 or num % 5 == 0]) print(f'answer: {answer}')
